Pauline Hawkes

At a slow pace 15 minute walk from Edmonton Green station I met Pauline Hawkins in her offices at Phoenix Community Centre. She had a large green jumper on, a big smile and an enormous sense of hospitality. From that moment onwards I knew that we would get along well. She asked me where I was from, I responded “Norway”. She got all fidgety and said “Oh, Ok! I won't ask too many questions then. As my husband has projects in Norway and he would also like the hear the answers to all our questions.

Adrian, Pauline´s husband drove us to the overground station and just as she suspected asked all the questions about Norway with big interest in Askim, the place god forgot. He asked vigorously where I was from, where I grew up. He really enjoys Norway. Pauline not so much, as she is a Londoner and loves the big city life.
